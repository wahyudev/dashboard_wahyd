Abstract

This activity based on the Kotodian and Limau Manis communities (ex DesaPulauTengah) who are using water springs at the downhill as a source of daily clean water. The problems that occur in water distribution to home resident is water distribution is uneven. Pipes used to deliver water to the houses are not comparable with the discharge flowing water, which is only 3 inches . In addition, another problem that arises is the lack of awareness of the community to get steady stream of water. Moreover, the problem is that people do not use water faucet to controll flow of water, so that when not used there is so much water is wasted. As a consequence, residents do not get water downstream . The use of water machine was necessary for those who are lack of water. Therefore, proper management is required in the distribution of clean water so that the water can be enjoyed by all community.
